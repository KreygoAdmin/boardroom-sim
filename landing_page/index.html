How it works

Any Conversation in Three Steps

From idea to a live multi-agent chat in under a minute.

1

Build Your Cast

Create agents with custom names, personalities, roles, and AI models. Use a template, build manually, or describe what you want in plain English and let the AI Builder assemble it for you.

2

Set the Scene

Drop a question, topic, or scenario into the chat. An orchestrator assigns the most fitting agent to respond first, keeping the conversation natural and in character throughout.

3

Watch Them Talk

Your agents debate, agree, push back, and build on each other's ideas — while notes, votes, and summaries are captured automatically. You can jump in at any time.

Features

Built for Real Group Dynamics

Not just parallel queries — agents that actually respond to each other.

🧠

Mix Any AI Models

Power each agent with a different model — Gemini, Claude, GPT-4o, or anything on OpenRouter. Different models think differently — lean into it.

📋

Live Meeting Notes

Consensus points, friction areas, momentum, and action items are tracked and updated automatically as the discussion evolves.

🗳️

Formal Voting

The orchestrator triggers votes when the group is ready — binary YES/NO or multi-option. Results are logged with a per-agent breakdown.

🔍

Live Web Research

When factual questions arise, a research agent fires a Google Search query and feeds real context back to the group before they respond.

🖊️

Shared Whiteboard

A scratchpad all agents can reference. Set agendas, drop context, or take notes — it persists with the room.

🎧

Voice Playback

Each agent can speak in their own ElevenLabs voice. Audio plays in sequence and can be paused at any time.

⚡

Auto-mode

Let the conversation run on its own. Pair it with live research for deep, self-directed analysis on any topic — no prompting needed.

📄

Full Reports

Export a complete Markdown report at any time: all messages, vote results, notes summary, and action items — ready to copy or share.

📊

Alignment Tracking

Each agent's alignment bar updates live to show how much they agree with the current direction of the conversation.

Tips

Get the Most From Your Room

Make personalities clash The more differentiated your agents are, the richer and more interesting the conversation. An optimist and a cynic in the same room is always more useful than two agreeable voices.
Use the whiteboard as context Set the scene before the first message — background info, constraints, a goal. It gives every agent a shared frame to work from.
Combine Auto-mode + Live Research For complex topics, let the group dig in on their own with real-time search grounding. You'll be surprised how deep it goes.
Brief mode for rapid iteration Toggle Brief in the toolbar for fast, punchy responses. Turn it off when you want deeper analysis or longer arguments.
Trigger votes naturally Just describe a motion in the chat — the orchestrator will detect it and open a formal vote without you having to do anything special.
